Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

CropSense is a platform designed to provide farmers with personalized farming recommendations based on soil type, weather conditions, and crop types.

You can register as a farmer by visiting the registration page, filling in your details (name, email, soil type, etc.), and submitting the form. Once registered, you can log in to access personalized recommendations.

Once you are logged in, you can navigate to your profile and update your preferences (e.g., crops, soil type, location). The system will automatically adjust the recommendations based on your updated preferences.
